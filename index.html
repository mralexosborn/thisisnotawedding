Dress Code

Formal, avant garde

We're asking for formal attire because this is a celebration worth dressing up for. But we encourage interpretation.

Embrace the unexpected. A melting pocket square. An impossible color. Accessories that shouldn't exist. The only rule: come as the most elegant version of yourself.

Some ideas

  • Floor-length gowns, cocktail dresses
  • Tuxedos, dark suits
  • Surrealist accessories encouraged
  • Metallics, jewel tones, chartreuse
  • Comfortable dancing shoes

Please avoid

  • White or ivory (that's Meg's territory)
  • Casual attire
  • Anything you couldn't dance in

FAQ

Questions you might have

Wait, so there's no ceremony?

Correct. We are having a private ceremony earlier in the day. This party is purely a celebration.

Can I bring a date?

Yes, every invitee is entitled to a guest. Formal invitations will be sent at a later date.

What's the parking situation?

The Science Museum has a large free parking lot. Ubers and Lyfts are also plentiful in the area.

Will there be an open bar?

Yes. We take hospitality seriously.

What if I have dietary restrictions?

Please let us know when you RSVP and we'll make sure you're taken care of. Our caterer is excellent with accommodations.
